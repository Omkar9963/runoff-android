How the model works +
Runoff is computed daily with the Modified SCS Curve Number method. Curve numbers come from MODIS land cover and OpenLandMap soil texture (grouped into hydrologic soil groups), then adjusted for slope from the SRTM DEM. A five-day antecedent-rainfall window shifts each cell between dry, normal and wet states (AMC I / II / III) before solving the runoff depth.
Data sources +
CHIRPS, daily precipitation
NEX-GDDP-CMIP6, climate projections
MODIS MCD12Q1, land cover
OpenLandMap, soil texture
SRTM, elevation & slope
Reading the output +
The map shows cumulative depth over the period; readouts are the district-wide spatial average. The daily series keeps every day. Export the runoff grid as a georeferenced GeoTIFF for GIS.
